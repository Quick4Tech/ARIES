Italian Village Pizza

207 W High St, Ebensburg, PA 15931 | (814) 472-2202

Description

A favorite of residents all throughout Ebensburg from the SGA offices to the local students hanging out before buying a vape on the block over. It's got a very wide selection with everything from pizza to salad to wings.

Review

Can't really go wrong with a good order from IVP, they are always reliable with the quality of their food. The salads are a little pricey, but our testers conclude they are a highlight of the menu.
